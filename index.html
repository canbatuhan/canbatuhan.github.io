about

Born in 2000 at Bursa, Turkey. Graduated from Ahmet Erdem Anatolian High School in 2018. Received Bachelor's Degree from Istanbul Technical University Department of Computer Engineering in Spring 2023. Started Master's Degree in the same department in Fall 2023. Studies focus on Distributed Systems and Edge Architecture. Works in Istanbul Technical University Faculty of Computer and Informatics Engineering as a research assistant. Also a member of ITU Cloud Computing Research Laboratory (Software Engineering Laboratory as its "thematic" name).
